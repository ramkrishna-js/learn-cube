### How to use
1.  **Scramble:** Follow the moves at the top (White top, Green front) to mix your cube.
2.  **Inspect:** Plan your cross.
3.  **Start:** Click the numbers (or tap on mobile) to start the timer.
4.  **Solve:** Go fast!
5.  **Stop:** Click anywhere on the timer area to stop.
