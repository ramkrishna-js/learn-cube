## 1. White Cross
*   **Daisy:** Yellow center, white petals -> Move to bottom.
*   **Flipped Edge:** `F' U L' U'`

## 2. First Layer Corners
*   **Right:** `R U R'`
*   **Front:** `U' R' U' R`
*   **Up:** `R U2 R' U' R U R'`

## 3. Second Layer Edges
*   **Right:** `U R U' R' U' F' U F`
*   **Left:** `U' L' U L U F U' F'`

## 4. Last Layer
*   **Cross (Dot/Line):** `F R U R' U' F'`
*   **Cross (L-Shape):** `F U R U' R' F'`
*   **Match Edges:** `R U R' U R U2 R' U`
*   **Position Corners:** `U R U' L' U R' U' L`
*   **Twist Corners:** `R' D' R D` (Repeat!)
