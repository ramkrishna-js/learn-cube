Welcome to **Learn Cube**!

The Rubik's Cube is one of the world's most famous puzzles. It might look impossible at first, with 43 quintillion possible combinations, but it can be solved by anyone using a sequence of logical steps.

## What you will learn

1.  **The Mechanics:** How the pieces move and interact.
2.  **The Notation:** The language we use to describe moves.
3.  **The Solution:** Step-by-step algorithms to return the cube to its solved state.

## Prerequisites

You don't need to be a math genius. You just need:
*   A Rubik's Cube (3x3).
*   Patience.
*   Practice.

Ready? Let's start by learning the language of the cube in the [Notation Guide](/guides/notation/).
