Cubing is a community. Don't solve alone.

## Databases (Algorithms)
*   **[SpeedCubeDB](https://speedcubedb.com):** The bible of algorithms. Lists every OLL, PLL, and ZBLL with votes on which ones are best.
*   **[AlgDb.net](http://algdb.net):** Classic resource.

## Timers
*   **[cstimer.net](https://cstimer.net):** The gold standard. Customizable, supports all puzzles, connects to hardware timers.
*   **[Twisty Timer](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.h3ss.twistytimer):** Best Android app.

## Communities
*   **[r/Cubers](https://reddit.com/r/cubers):** The biggest forum. Great for "Daily Discussion" and advice.
*   **[SpeedSolving Forums](https://www.speedsolving.com/):** The old-school forum where legends hang out.
*   **[WCA (World Cube Association)](https://www.worldcubeassociation.org):** Sign up for competitions here.

## YouTube Channels
*   **J Perm:** The best tutorials on the internet. Clear, concise, perfect graphics.
*   **CubeHead:** Entertainment + high-level solving.
*   **Tingman:** Wholesome, family-friendly reviews and skits.
